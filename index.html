Classical in New Tech

Elements is a book app. It is designed for students and teachers. Elements redesigns the classical geometry book - Euclid's Elements of Geometry in a new way. With the new technology supporting, it is showing a vivid way to display the great mind contained in Euclid's Elements of Geometry.

Interactive Demonstration

In this book app, the demonstrations are interactive, that are providing a easy way for you to control the geometry objects, like change the length of a segment, move a point form one place to another and so on. It makes geometry learing interesting.

Real Simulation

Relationship between geometry objects in the demonstrations will be real show out. Following the radius of a circle changing, the size of this circle will change at the same time.
